What is the AT Protocol?

The Authenticated Transfer (AT) Protocol is a new foundation for decentralized social media. Unlike traditional platforms, it is designed to give users account portability, algorithmic choice, and interoperation.

In short: it allows different social networks to communicate with each other, while letting you keep your identity and data if you decide to switch providers.
